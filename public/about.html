Kevin Simonnet

Kevin is the Co-founder, CEO and CPO of CrawlKeep, with over 15 years of experience in Talent Acquisition and Executive Search.

"It all started with developing an Excel VBA app to automate my Executive Search / Talent Acquisition job. I launched exeksourcing.com. It got me to step into the gig economy. As a Freelancer, I've been offering startups Executive Search grade quality at a fraction of the price.

The digitalisation of Talent Mapping / Sourcing dramatically expanded:

  • going from 800 profiles to tens of thousands per week
  • whilst cutting delivery timeframes, from 3 weeks to 5 days
  • analyse large amount of Talent data (+2TBs), we could enable data-driven decision-making to provide easily digestible market & Talent insights (e.g. competitors’ landscape, organizational chart, seniority pyramid, talent trends, compensation benchmark)
  • Through dynamic visualization

Next, I met André, a Senior Program Manager at Amazon, who had developed a Chrome extension to crawl a famous professional network. Together, we expanded it to other social networks and job boards. I learnt HTML, CSS and NodeJS.

Encouraged by its success, I embarked on a journey to migrate it into a web application using Node.js. That's where the idea to invert the process came:
Empower professionals to proactively headhunt their next employers!"
